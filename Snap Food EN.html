Learn more about Snap Food!

Snapfood is a new culture of ordering food that has been introduced and implemented for the first time in Iran. Ordering food online in Iran was only a dream before the launch of Snapfood, and few people knew about its benefits. This dream became a reality in 2008 and during this time it has gained many fans among the people of Iran. Snapfood is the first Iranian website owned by Avanagar Atlas Tejarat company that has brought online food ordering to the people of Iran and has provided many facilities for the convenience of more people and restaurants and will add to these facilities day by day. Our users can easily choose the food they want and select one of the three payment methods and finally receive their food.
